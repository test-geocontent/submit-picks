Our Thesis

Why We're Doing This

"Microcap conferences are full of interesting companies, but most investors never have a structured way to track which ideas actually work."

This contest is designed to make the process more fun, more transparent, and more competitive. We want to bring accountability and structure to a space that's often driven by intuition alone.

It's also a way to spotlight real stock-picking skill in the microcap investing community—and reward people who put in the work.
